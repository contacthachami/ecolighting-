1. Visit: [https://www.qr-code-generator.com/](https://www.qr-code-generator.com/)
2. Enter your deployed URL
3. **Customize**:
   - Upload `logo.Ecolighting.png` in center
   - Use green (#0a6b3c) for QR blocks
   - Use yellow (#ffcc00) for accents
   - Add frame text: "Scan to Connect with Ecolighting"
4. Download high-resolution (300 DPI minimum)

### Recommended QR Code Sizes:

- Business Cards: 2.5cm x 2.5cm
- Flyers/Posters: 5cm x 5cm
- Banners: 10cm x 10cm or larger
